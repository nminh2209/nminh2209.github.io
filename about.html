I'm Nguyen Hoang Minh — a Software Engineering student at Swinburne University of Technology (graduating 2026) with a passion for building real-world, production-ready software. My journey is built on two pillars: code that works at scale, and music that moves people.

Over the past year I've worked across the full stack: from backend APIs with FastAPI and Django, to polished React frontends with TypeScript and Vite, to real-time systems on Firebase. I've maintained a live production CRM platform handling 10,000+ daily events, instrumented Azure Application Insights across three vendors, and fixed critical async patterns with exponential backoff retry achieving 99.9% success rates.

Beyond software, I'm a classically trained pianist — self-taught over many years — and a lifelong Final Fantasy fan. Music taught me something that code reinforced: that mastery is not about knowing everything, but about caring enough to get the details right. The same discipline that makes a Chopin étude clean makes a production deployment reliable.

I'm open to opportunities in full-stack development, DevOps, and AI-driven projects. If you're building something that matters, I'd love to talk.
