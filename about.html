I'm Nguyen Hoang Minh — a Software Engineering student at Swinburne University of Technology (graduating 2026), building production software from Hanoi. I care about systems that stay up, code that other people can maintain, and the small details that separate a demo from something a team can rely on.

Professionally, I've moved between solo builds and structured teams: a live bakery ERP still in production, a capstone where I owned backend architecture, production CRM work at MindX, Agile delivery at BlueOC, and from May 2026 an AI Engineering fellowship with Vingroup's AI Thực Chiến program at VinUniversity. The through-line is full-stack work with real constraints — security, CI, async failures, and honest deployment trade-offs. Portfolio has the case studies, commits, and repos.

Beyond software, I'm a self-taught pianist and a lifelong Final Fantasy fan. Music taught me something that code reinforced: mastery is not about knowing everything, but about caring enough to get the details right. The same discipline that makes a Chopin étude clean makes a production deployment reliable.

I'm open to opportunities in full-stack development, DevOps, and AI-driven projects. If you're building something that matters, I'd love to talk.
